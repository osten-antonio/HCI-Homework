Pepperoni Pizza

Indulge in our signature Pepperoni Pizza, featuring a hand-tossed, golden crust baked to perfection. Covered with a generous layer of rich, melted mozzarella cheese, this pizza is topped with thin slices of savory, zesty pepperoni that crisp up beautifully in the oven. Finished with a hint of Italian herbs and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil, this classic favorite delivers a deliciously bold and satisfying flavor in every bite.

Recipe ▼
Amount Ingredients
16 ounces Pizza dough
1/2 cup Pizza sauce
18 to 20 Pepperoni slices
1/2 tsp Ground black pepper
1 tsp Fresh oregano
Sufficient amount Flour (for rolling and shaping dough)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500°F or for 20 minutes if you are using pizza stone
  2. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface. Let it rest for 5 minutes if it hard to roll. Roll until 14-inch diameter circle
  3. Transfer the dough to a lightly dusted pizza peel, or fit it into a large cast-iron. Add sauce in a light layer all over the pizza, leaving a 1/4 inch space crust. Chop the pepperoni and sprinkle it all over. Top the pizza with grated cheese and the rest of the pepperoni. Season with black pepper.
  4. Cook the pizza for 6 minutes, then rotate the pizza halfway so it cooks evenly. Cook for another 6 to 8 minutes until the crust is golden brown and charred in spots
