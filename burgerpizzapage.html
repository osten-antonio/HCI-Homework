Cheese and Pepperoni Pizza burger

A fusion between a Pepperoni Pizza with a Cheese burger, with a beef patty in between. This meal mixes the best of both burgers and pizzas to produce a mouthwatering delight, featuring a juicy beef patty nestled between layers of mozzarela cheese, pepperoni slices, and a rich tomato sauce, all encased in a toasted bun thats infused with the flavors of pizza crust.

Recipe ▼
Amount Ingredients
Pizza
1 tsp Active-dry yeast
256 g All-purpose flour
3/4 tsp Salt
2 tsp Salt
Pizza toppings
120 ml Passata
Any amount Cheddar cheese
Any amount Mozzarella
Any amount Pepperoni
Burger
500g Mince beef
2 eggs
2 tbsp Oregano
1 tsp Garlic powder
1 tsp Paprika
1 tbsp Onion granuals
2 Sliced of bread blending into crumbs
Any amount Black pepper
Any amount Salt
100 ml Passata
Any amount Mozzarella sliced

Preparing burger patties

  1. Mix the Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the minced beef, eggs, garlic powder, paprika, onion granules, breadcrumbs, salt, and black pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Form the Patties: Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape them into burger patties.

Cooking the burger patties

  1. Preheat a Grill or Pan: Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. You can lightly oil the surface to prevent sticking.
  2. Cook the Patties: Add the patties to the grill or pan. Cook for about 4-5 minutes on one side, then flip.

Assemble the Pizza Burger

  1. Prepare the Buns: Toast the burger buns lightly on the grill or in a toaster, if desired.
  2. Layer the Ingredients: Place a cooked burger patty with melted cheese and toppings on the bottom half of each bun.
  3. Garnish (Optional): Add fresh basil or oregano for extra flavor, if desired.
  4. Top and Serve: Add the top half of the bun.
